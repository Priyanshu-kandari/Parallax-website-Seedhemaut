SEEDHE MAUT

Who is Seedhe maut?

Seedhe Maut is a hip-hop/rap duo from New Delhi, India, consisting of Encore ABJ (Abhijay Negi) and Calm (Siddhant Sharma). Known for their razor-sharp lyrics and raw storytelling, they bring an aggressive, no-filter style that reflects the realities of youth and urban life in India. Their music blends Hindi and English seamlessly, creating a unique desi rap identity.

They broke into the scene with their debut album Bayaan and gained a cult following through their high-energy performances and hard-hitting verses. With projects like Nayaab and collaborations with top Indian producers and rappers, Seedhe Maut has become one of the leading voices of India’s underground hip-hop revolution.

CAREER

Seedhe Maut began their journey in Delhi’s underground rap scene, where Encore ABJ and Calm met through battle rap circles. They gained early attention with Seedhe Maut Anthem and their 2018 debut album Bayaan, produced by Sez on the Beat. Their hard-hitting lyrics and bilingual flow quickly set them apart in the Indian hip-hop space, earning them a cult following and a deal with Azadi Records.

Over the years, they’ve evolved from underground icons to mainstream disruptors with projects like Nayaab, Lunch Break, and their independent label DL91 Era. With a reputation for sharp verses, honest storytelling, and electric live shows, Seedhe Maut remains one of India’s most respected and influential rap duos.

VISION

Seedhe Maut’s vision is to redefine Indian hip-hop by staying authentic, fearless, and boundary-pushing. They aim to give voice to real, unfiltered stories—blending personal experiences, social commentary, and street realities in a language and style that resonates with young India.

Their goal goes beyond fame—they want to build a movement. By launching their own label (DL91 Era) and mentoring emerging talent, they’re creating space for underground artists to thrive independently, free from commercial pressure, while keeping the art form raw, powerful, and rooted in truth.
